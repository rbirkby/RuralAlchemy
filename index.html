Alchemy: A magical process of transformation: combination; creation. We're mixologists ... with a difference.

We're resolutely rural: we embrace local life ~ our trees, barns, pubs and suppers ~ cosily sipping a glass of something warm and red, or fresh and bubbly. But we ALSO want to create events you'd LOVE LOVE LOVE, if you lived in big smoke: you just don't want the hassle of the train, the taxi, the babysitter, and quite frankly, all those people!

We want fun. We want exclusivity. But we want it on our doorstep!

So if you're an eager beaver, get in touch and we'll be offering our first new friends, a big fat discount for our brand new members' club.
